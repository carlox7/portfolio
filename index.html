About Me:

Hi my name is Carlo and I am an aspiring web developer. I attended the University of Washington to pursue a journalism degree. Coming out of high school I always thought that writing for a tech or game publication was the career that I wanted to pursue. In that time studying journalism I always had an interest in the internet and web development, but I never thought that it was something that I could do. After some time I decided to give it a try and it brought me to Code Fellows. I'm only in the beginning stage of my journey but I feel like web development has opened my eyes not only to a whole new world and career path, but it also gives me a chance to come full circle and work on the technology side of a tech or game publication in the future.
